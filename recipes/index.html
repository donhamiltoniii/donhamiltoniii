Salads

  • Creamy Avocado and Cucumber Salad

    Throw half-mooned cucumbers, cubed avocado, and thinly sliced scallions in a bowl. Dress with olive oil, lime juice, and flaky salt, and toss to coat.

  • Almost-Greek Cucumber and Cherry Tomato Salad

    Toss halved cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumber, and chunks of feta with olive oil and salt.

  • A BBQ-Worthy Quick-Pickled Cucumber Salad

    Dress finely diced chives and sliced cucumber with cider vinegar, olive oil, and a pinch each of sugar and salt.
